Crowdsourced Brain Mapping

--------------------------

There are lots of ways to map the brain. This database puts a crowdsourced layer of metadata onto research papers. The goal is to help neuroscientists share and find relevant data that use different mapping methodologies or terminologies (nomeclatures).

To help out, take a look at these research paper databases. Make sure to right click and "copy the link" to paste it into the URL field, or else you won't be able to get the URL easily.

  • Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (Neuroscience)
  • Nature Neuroscience
  • Neuron, Cell Press
